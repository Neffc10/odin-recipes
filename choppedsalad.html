Chopped Salad

Description

A simple salad that balances crispy lettuce and cucumber with creamy egg and avocado

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Bacon
  • Blue Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Crisp Bacon in pan while boiling eggs just under hard (6-8 minutes)
  2. Roughly chop lettuce, tomato, and eggs, slice cucumber and avocado
  3. Combine prepped ingredients and lightly dress with oil,vinegar,salt and pepper
  4. Top with crumbled bacon and blue cheese and serve
