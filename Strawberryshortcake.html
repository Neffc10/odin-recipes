Strawberry Shortcake

Description

White cake with strawberry and whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • Sponge Cake, Pound Cake or any White Cake
  • Fresh Strawberry
  • Sugar
  • Heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Lightly coat strawberry in sugar and refrigerate
  2. Mix cream and sugar in a stand mixer with a whisk attatchment then refrigerate
  3. Top sliced cake with strawberry followed by whipped cream then top with additional strawberry
